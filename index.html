WELCOME TO
AREWA RENNAISANCE COLLEGE

Arewa Renaissance is envisioned to be a multi-campus basic education institution. It provides educational and related services for pre-primary, primary, secondary and pre-university students. It’s planned to start with one junior and one senior secondary school classes. ARC will start in its purpose-built accommodation in Kano. Later, it will expand in Kano and other states.

MISSION

To focus on outcome-based teaching, learning and moral uprightness; to assist students and graduates to realize their full potential in dynamic multicultural settings in the global village, while retaining ...their Islamic values and identity; to use modern technology for bridging the wide gap in quality, accessibility and affordability of education among the different classes and regions of Nigeria.

VISION

ARC seek to develop nd nature strong, balanced personalities with self confidence in Islamic values and being critical and creative thinker towards achieving excellence.

EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES

ARC will produce graduates to have:

  • intellectual abilities with balanced personalities
  • leadership abilities in multicultural settings
  • strong communication skills and competencies in using modern technological tools
